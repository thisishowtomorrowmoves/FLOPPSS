Billie Eilish

American Singer-Songwriter | 7x Grammy Award Winner

About Billie

Billie Eilish is an American singer-songwriter who rose to fame with her debut single "Ocean Eyes" in 2015. Known for her unique sound and hauntingly beautiful vocals, Billie has become one of the most influential and iconic artists of her generation. Her music blends pop, alternative, and electronic genres, resonating with millions around the world.

Career Highlights

  • Debut album *When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?* (2019) became a global hit
  • Winner of 7 Grammy Awards in 2020, including Album of the Year, Record of the Year, and Song of the Year
  • Released *Happier Than Ever* (2021), which also topped charts worldwide
  • Known for her activism, fashion sense, and advocacy for mental health awareness

Discography

  • When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go? (2019) - Featuring hits like "Bad Guy"
  • Happier Than Ever (2021) - Featuring hits like "Your Power" and "Therefore I Am"
  • Singles - "Ocean Eyes" (2015), "Bury a Friend" (2019), and many more.
